Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance across multiple product lines. <br>
Utilized DAX formulas to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Total Costs, transactions, total sales, conversion rates, Total Profits, and sales growth. <br>
INSIGHTS DERIVED <BR><BR>
 1) Identified high-margin and low-margin products, enabling the sales team to focus on promoting products with the highest potential for revenue generation. <BR>
 2) Analyzed regional sales data to identify geographic areas with the highest growth potential and those requiring additional attention. <br>
 3) Uncovered seasonal trends and patterns in sales, aiding in the development of targeted marketing campaigns during peak seasons.
